Of Mice and MEGA MEN

If you don't like a ginormous, 50 foot, half naked, tattered diaper wearing man battling the US Army in downtown Las Vegas then you don't like B-Movies my friend!

The latest project at AB Design Co is a poster book commemorating the most ridiculous B-Movies of all time. We've all flipped past a grotesque irradiated alien insect horde obliterating the people of earth at 4 a.m. while searching for something, anything, to watch.

these crabs are making me thirsty

This book is for the people who choose to say, "What the fuuu..." And switch back to that channel, taking absolute joy in the camp and melodrama of mind-controlled crabs descending on the white house to kidnap the president.

Don't get it twisted, these movies were chosen based SOLELY on their posters
