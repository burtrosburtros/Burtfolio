The Kurosawa Collection

Akira Kurosawa, master sensei of the samurai epic, was praised for his ability to inspire powerful and memorable performances from his lead actors

It was important to showcase those distinct performances, and the personalities delivering them, in a unique way for this bluray collection

Requirements:

capture the essence of lead actors in a hirsute style, use distinct palette for each cover, style should be consistent for box set

Kurosawa painted fantastical tales of wandering ronin warriors seeking fulfillment and retribution across the sweeping mountain vistas of Japan

Now you can enjoy these tales of grey morality, disguised royalty, and war torn family disputes drenched in blood, in glorious HD, in your living room
